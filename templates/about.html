About Elder Med Manager

Our Mission

Elder Med Manager is designed to help healthcare professionals, caregivers, and elderly patients better manage complex medication regimens. Our tool identifies potential risks and provides actionable recommendations to improve medication safety and adherence.

Key Features

  • Medication burden calculation for patients 65+
  • Beers Criteria assessment (2023 AGS guidelines)
  • Drug interaction checking
  • Fall risk assessment
  • Anticholinergic burden scoring
  • Medication adherence prediction
  • Simplification recommendations

Disclaimer
